Our Mission

Every website you visit collects something about you. Privacy policies exist to tell you what they collect, how they use it, and who they share it with — but they are written in legalese, buried behind links, and almost no one reads them.

Privacy Policy Tracker was built to change that. Our goal is to make online privacy transparent, accessible, and actionable for everyone — not just lawyers and security experts.

What we do

We automatically find, summarize, and track privacy policies so you can see at a glance how a website handles your data. When policies change, we let you know. Our browser extension brings this information directly into your workflow, right where you browse.

What we believe

Privacy is a fundamental right. Understanding how your data flows online shouldn't require a law degree or hours of reading. Everyone deserves to know what companies know about them — and to make informed choices about where they spend their time and attention on the internet.

We're building tools that put that power back in your hands, one privacy policy at a time.
