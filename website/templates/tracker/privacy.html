Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

At Privacy Policy Tracker, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Data we collect

  • URLs you submit — When you enter a website URL to track its privacy policy, we store that URL associated with your account.
  • Privacy policy content — We scrape and store the privacy policies of websites you track for summarization and change detection.
  • Account information — If you create an account, we store your email address and a password hash.
  • Usage data — We collect basic analytics (page views, feature usage) to improve the service.

How we use your data

  • To generate and update summaries of privacy policies you track
  • To notify you when tracked privacy policies change
  • To improve our summarization models and service quality
  • To communicate important service updates

Data we do not collect

  • We do not track your browsing history or activity beyond URLs you explicitly submit
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do not share your tracked URLs with advertisers

Your browser extension

Our browser extension runs entirely in your browser. It does not send your browsing data to our servers unless you explicitly choose to submit a URL for tracking. The extension communicates with our servers only to display summaries of privacy policies you have opted to track.

Data retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, and we will process it within 30 days.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us through our project repository.
