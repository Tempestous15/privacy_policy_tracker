Our Mission

Every website you visit collects something about you. Privacy policies exist to tell you what they collect, how they use it, and who they share it with — but they are written in legalese, buried behind links, and almost no one reads them.

ClipPri was built to change that. Our goal is to make online privacy transparent, accessible, and actionable for everyone — not just lawyers and security experts.

What we do

Our browser extension automatically finds a site's privacy policy and scans it for red flags — data selling, vague third-party sharing, indefinite retention, arbitration clauses, and more — the moment you land on the page. Where available, it also surfaces community-reviewed ratings from ToS;DR, and can generate a plain-English summary using a small language model that runs entirely on your device.

What we believe

Privacy is a fundamental right. Understanding how your data flows online shouldn't require a law degree or hours of reading. Everyone deserves to know what companies know about them — and to make informed choices about where they spend their time and attention on the internet.

That belief extends to how the extension itself works: everything runs locally, in your browser. We don't operate a server that sees what you browse, and we never see the policies you scan.
