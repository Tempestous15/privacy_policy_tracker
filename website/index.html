Know what a site does with your data before you agree to anything

ClipPri is a browser extension that reads a site's privacy policy for you — instantly, entirely on your device. No account, no server, nothing you read ever leaves your browser.

Get the extension Learn more

🚩 Instant red flags

A local, on-device scan flags data selling, vague third-party sharing, indefinite retention, arbitration clauses, and more — the moment you open a page.

🌐 ToS;DR ratings

Where available, community-reviewed ratings from Terms of Service; Didn't Read are shown alongside the local scan for well-known sites.

🧠 On-device AI summary

A small language model runs locally in your browser (via WebGPU) to generate a plain-English summary — no policy text is ever sent to us or anyone else.
